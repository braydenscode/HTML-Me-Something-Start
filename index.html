Top 5 Story Games (2019-2024)

A collection of my top 5 story games from the last 5 years.

(Ordered by release date)

Metro Exodus

Released to PC on February 15, 2019. Metro Exodus is a first-person shooter set in a post-apocalyptic world. Starting in Moscow's dangerous underground metro, you travel throughout the irradiated landscape of Russia after a nuclear war. Where you face many obstacles and threats from ghouls and survivors alike.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Released to PC on November 5, 2019. Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure game set in an extremely detailed world based on the American frontier in the late 1800s. You play as an outlaw surrounded by his Wild West posse, but the game gives you many moral decisions leaving it up to you what kind of reputation you want to acquire. Whether that be an honorable vigilante or a ruthless outlaw.

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Released to PC on November 15, 2019. Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an action-adventure game that takes place within the Star Wars universe. You play as a young Jedi who must evade the Empire and uncover ancient secrets about the fall of the Jedi while simultaneously mastering lightsaber combat and force powers.

God of War

Released to PC on January 14, 2022. God of War is an action-adventure game where you play as Kratos, the former Greek god. Who moved to this far-away Norse land to avoid the drama of the gods. Despite this, he is sucked into a world steeped in Norse mythology. There he faces the responsibility of raising his son while also navigating this dangerous world battling mythological creatures.

Ghost of Tsushima

Released to PC on May 16, 2024. Ghost of Tsushima is an open-world action game set in feudal Japan. You play as a young samurai faced with defending his home from the Mongol invasion. You soon discover that to stand a chance against the fierce Mongol invaders you'll need to use tactics like stealth that go against the honorable traditions of the samurai.

What these games have in common:

All of these games have beautiful graphics, satisfying gameplay, and intriguing storytelling. However, the main thing that I think ties all of these great games together is unique yet detailed worlds that help to immerse you within the game while you play.
